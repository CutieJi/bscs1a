About MIS eBorrow

MIS Equipment Management Through QR Code Technology

MIS eBorrow is a modern equipment borrowing and lending system designed to streamline the process of managing educational resources through intelligent QR code tracking and real-time monitoring.

Our Mission

To revolutionize equipment management in educational institutions by providing a seamless, efficient, and accountable system that enables students and staff to easily borrow and return equipment while maintaining complete transparency and real-time tracking.

Our Vision

To become the leading equipment management solution for educational institutions worldwide, setting new standards for accessibility, accountability, and efficiency in resource sharing through innovative technology and user-centered design.

What is MIS eBorrow?

A comprehensive solution for equipment management

MIS eBorrow is a web-based equipment borrowing system that uses QR code technology to simplify the process of lending and tracking educational equipment such as projectors, HDMI cables, power cords, remotes, and other classroom devices. Students can quickly scan QR codes to borrow or return items, while administrators maintain complete oversight through a powerful management dashboard.

How It Works

A simple three-step process

1

Scan QR Code

Students use their smartphone or device camera to scan the QR code attached to any equipment. The system instantly identifies the item and displays its current status and details.

2

Borrow Equipment

After scanning, students can immediately borrow available equipment by selecting a return date and specifying the purpose. The system logs the transaction with complete details including timestamp and user information.

3

Return & Track

When finished, students scan the QR code again to return the equipment. They can rate the condition and add notes. Administrators can track all activities, view analytics, and manage overdue items.
