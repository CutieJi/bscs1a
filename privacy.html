Privacy Policy

This page explains what information MIS eBorrow collects, how it is used, and how we protect it.

🔎 Information We Collect

  • Account info: name, email, role (student/admin), student ID (students only).
  • Borrowing records: equipment borrowed, date/time, return time, room, purpose, condition, notes.
  • System activity: logs used for auditing and monitoring usage.

🧠 Why We Collect It

  • To manage borrowing: track who borrowed what and when.
  • To ensure accountability: overdue monitoring and equipment condition tracking.
  • To improve service: detect issues and improve system experience.

🔐 Data Protection

  • We store data securely using Firebase Authentication and Firestore database rules.
  • Only admins can view all users and full borrowing logs.
  • Students can only access their own borrowing records.

🗑 Data Retention

  • Borrowing logs may be kept for school reporting and auditing purposes.
  • Users may request account removal from the system (handled by admin).

📨 Contact

If you have privacy concerns, please contact the administrator via the Support page.

⚠️ Notes

  • Do not share your password with anyone.
  • Use demo/test accounts only for development.
