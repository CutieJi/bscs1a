Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 29, 2026. This policy describes how MISLend handles your personal and system data.

Information We Process

  • Account Identification: We collect your full name, institutional email address, and Student ID to verify eligibility.
  • Service Data: Borrowing records, including item IDs, timestamps, and locations, are stored to manage the equipment lifecycle.
  • Technical Logs: Basic device information and system interaction logs are used for security and troubleshooting.

Service Providers

MISLend utilizes Google Firebase for backend infrastructure.

  • Authentication: Securely manages login credentials.
  • Firestore: Stores encrypted system data and records.
  • Hosting: Provides the web application interface.

Security Measures

  • Access Control: Data is protected by Firestore Security Rules, ensuring students only see their own records.
  • Encryption: Data in transit and at rest is encrypted using industry-standard protocols.
  • Admin Oversight: Only authorized MIS personnel have access to system-wide logs.

Your Rights

  • Access: View your borrowing history at any time through your dashboard.
  • Correction: Request updates to inaccurate profile information via an administrator.
  • Deletion: Accounts and associated personal data can be removed upon graduation or request.

Data Retention

Records are retained for the duration of the academic semester for auditing. Academic-related borrowing history may be archived for system performance analysis but will be anonymized where possible.

Security Responsibility

Users are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of their credentials. MISLend will never ask for your password via email or SMS.
