WHO WE ARE

MISLend

MISLend was created by five BSCS 1A students from the University of Caloocan City. The system was born out of a real frustration: managing classroom equipment like projectors, HDMI cables, and extension cords through handwritten logbooks was slow, error-prone, and hard to audit. MISLend replaces all of that with a Firebase-powered web application where students scan a QR code on any equipment, submit a borrow request, and administrators approve or reject it in real time. Every transaction is timestamped and exportable to Excel.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our borrowing process

×
1

Who can borrow equipment?

Only currently enrolled UCC MIS students and verified faculty members can use MISLend.

2

How long can I keep an item?

Standard items like projectors and cables can be borrowed for the duration of a single class block (typically 3-4 hours).

3

What happens if I return equipment late?

Late returns are flagged in the system. Accumulating records of overdue returns may lead to temporary suspension of your borrowing privileges.

4

Do I need an ID?

Yes. Presenting your valid UCC Student or Faculty ID to the admin is required upon picking up the equipment.
