FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

  • Q: How do I reset my password?
    A: Currently, password resets are handled by administrators. Please contact the MIS Office.
  • Q: Can I borrow multiple items?
    A: Yes, as long as the items are available and you provide the room and return time for each.
  • Q: What if I return an item late?
    A: Late returns are flagged in the system. Please return items by the specified time to avoid penalties.
  • Q: My department is not UCC MIS, can I still use this?
    A: This system is specifically integrated for UCC MIS equipment. Contact your department for their own management tools.

Common Troubleshooting

  • Camera Permissions: If the QR scanner will not start, check your browser site settings and make sure camera access is allowed.
  • Network Requirements: QR scanning and real-time updates require an active internet connection and should be served over HTTPS.
  • Login Issues: Ensure your caps lock is off and you are using the correct Student ID format.
