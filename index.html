Why Choose MISLend

Why Use Our System?

Managing classroom equipment at UCC MIS (University of Caloocan City - Management Information Systems) used to mean paper forms, manual logs, and constant miscommunication. MISLend was built to eliminate all of that. With instant QR scanning, real-time tracking, and role-based dashboards, every borrow transaction is transparent, fast, and fully accountable - from request to return. We provide the tools to let you focus on your classes.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our borrowing process

×
1

Who can borrow equipment?

Only currently enrolled UCC MIS students and verified faculty members can use MISLend.

2

How long can I keep an item?

Standard items like projectors and cables can be borrowed for the duration of a single class block (typically 2-3 hours).

3

What happens if I return equipment late?

Late returns are flagged in the system. Accumulating records of overdue returns may lead to temporary suspension of your borrowing privileges.

4

Do I need an ID?

Yes. Presenting your valid UCC Student or Faculty ID to the admin is required upon picking up the equipment.
